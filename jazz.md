# Jazz
date: 2023-11-02

When I go to Japan these days, my regular schedule to spend every night (sometimes afternoons) at a jazz bar.

I have a list of:
   * a dozen or two regular places that I like to go to often
   * a list of probaby 100+ more than I go to once in a while, maybe once per trip
   * a list of new places that I want to check out at least once
   
Believe it or not, even with a trip of up to 90 days, sometimes I feel like I don't have enough time to check off my checklist.

During a recent trip, I went to 105 shows including on my arrival night (updating a spreadsheet & database tracking all the jazz events I've been to).

Here are some examples of typical jazz bars in Tokyo:
   * [Cochi](http://www.jazz-cochi.com/)
   * [Yoyogi Naru](https://yoyogi-naru.com/)
   * [Independence](http://jazz-independence.com/calendar/)
   * [The Deep](https://jazz-thedeep.com/)
   * [Sometime](https://www.sometime.co.jp/sometime/)
   * [Body & Soul](https://www.bodyandsoul.co.jp/)
