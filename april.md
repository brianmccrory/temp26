# April
date: 2023-11-02

April weather was great, clear skies and little rain. 

I usually wore a light fleece or sweater when going outside, but it wasn't really that cold compared to Feb. A light cashmere cap was also pretty useful.

There was some rain a few days in late April but this could happen anytime.
