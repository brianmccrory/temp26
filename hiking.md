# Hiking
date: 2023-11-02

Mount Takao ([Takao-san](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Takao)) is a great day-trip mountain hiking, pretty easy, with a great picnic area you can also eat and drink eat on one of the peaks. There is also a new onsen/restaurant facility nearby which some hikers use to unwind and relax after the climb.

Although it's referred to as 'mountain climbing', it's really more of an easy hike, uphill but not very hard.

Further out north of Tokyo is the great [Yamadera](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yama-dera) (mountain temple), a very memorable hike up a mountain to a secluded Buddhist temple. This would take 3- to 4-hours train travel (one way) and most likely one night's stay at a local hotel.
