# Visa and entry process

Tourists from the USA won’t need to apply for any special Visa to enter Japan. There’s a visa waiver stamp you get at the airport immigration, like an on-demand Visa stamp on entry, for 90-days maximum.

But to make the entry easier (immigration, customs), there is “Visit Japan Web” where you can register your trip details at https://www.vjw.digital.go.jp

This makes the entry process smooth and digital-based, meaning no paper forms to fill out and submit in Japan upon entering. But you will need your smartphone and a QR code to go through the lines smoothly.

To avoid roaming charges, if you have a compatible phone, you can install an esim (like Ubigi) and buy a small data package which kicks in when you arrive in Japan. This way, you can avoid any roaming charges from your US carrier, which may or may not work overseas.
