# Busy Season
date: 2023-11-03

When do most people travel to Japan from the US? If more people are traveling, then flights and hotels may get more expensive, and there may be more sightseeing crowds and longer lines.

I got this information from ANA airline's [Seasonality Charts](https://www.ana.co.jp/en/jp/guide/amc/award/international/terms/): 	

### Low Season (2024):
  * January 4 to February 29
  * April 1 to April 24
		
### Regular Season (2024):
  * March 1 to March 31
  * May 13 to July 18
  * August 26 to December 15
	
### High Season (2024):
   * January 1 to January 3
   * April 25 to May 12
   * July 19 to August 25
   * December 16 to December 31

There are separate charts for travel between Japan and US, vs Japan and Europe, Japan and Oceana, etc... but the high-season intervals are roughly the same.
