# Daily Costs
date: 2023-11-02

At this point, my estimate is to plan to spend around 10,000 (一万円, *ichi man yen*, one thousand yen) or up to $100 per day, per person, to cover:
   * bar costs & 1-2 drinks
   * an economical lunch and dinner (sometimes dinner is just whatever the jazz bar happens to have, but not all jazz bars serve food...)

Some bars are more expensive, 2x to 3x the above, but I go to those places less often.

Hotels, train tickets, coffee, snacks, tourist sites, souvenirs, purchases, etc would all be additional.
