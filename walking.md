# Walking
date: 2023-11-13

When sightseeing in Japan, there is a lot of walking between sites and stations, with plenty of stairs and often luggage or bags in tow.

Before you arrive in Japan, getting in the habit of daily walking and exercising your feet, legs, and overall body will pay off. Just walking around the neighborhood for 30 minutes every day is a great start.
Also be sure to take some comfortable shoes with you on your trip.
