# Hotels
date: 2023-11-02

Tokyo is a metropolis with lots of cities or districts (like large neighborhoods) connected by roads, trains, subways, and buses.
The main central area of Tokyo is made up of 23 of these wards, to give you a sense of scale, but there are even more cities that are part of or near to Tokyo, outside of this core. 

I usually rent an apt in Shinjuku, and my favorite recommended hotels are in this district, but other districts are great/popular, too: Shibuya, Roppongi, Asakusa, etc.

You can book directly from the hotel sites, or use sites like Expedia, Hotels.com, Hotwire, etc for possible discounts. 

In Shinjuku, from budget to extravagant (small sample of hotels I used to used that are around the area I always stay):

1. [Shinjuku Washington Hotel](https://shinjuku.washington-hotels.jp/)
1. [Sunroute Plaza](https://sotetsu-hotels.com/en/sunroute/plazashinjuku/)
1. [Keio Plaza](https://www.keioplaza.com/)
1. [Shinjuku Hilton ("Hilton Tokyo")](https://www.hilton.com/en/locations/japan/shinjuku-ku/)
1. [Shinjuku Hyatt Regency](https://www.hyatt.com/en-US/hotel/japan/hyatt-regency-tokyo/tyoty)
1. [Shinjuku Park Hyatt (famous in the movie *Lost in Translation*)](https://www.hyatt.com/en-US/hotel/japan/park-hyatt-tokyo/tyoph)
