# Seasons
date: 2023-11-03

I like Fall, Winter, Spring, Summer (in order).

| Season | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | 
| Fall | Comfortable weather; Leaves change colors | Typhoon season Aug/Sep |
| Winter | Holiday illumination and cheer | Many closures Jan 1-3; Can be very cold in Jan/Feb |
| Spring | Great weather; Cherry blossoms | Pollen allergies can be bad; Busy travel season Golden Week around May 1 |
| Summer | Beach weather; outdoor activies | Can be uncomfortably hot and humid; Rainy season Jun/Jul |
