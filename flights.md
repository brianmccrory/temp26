# Flights
date: 2023-11-02

I usually choose to fly on [ANA](https://www.ana.co.jp/en/us/) (mainly), but I have also used JAL (Japan Airlines), United, Air Canada, Singapore Airlines a few times.

These days, to accumuluate mileage and because I'm used to it, I expect to pay about $2300 (roundtrip) for a premium economy seat on ANA. I always buy my ticket from their website directly and don't have experience buying from travel sites like Expedia.

The most important things for me when flying have been:

* direct flight (most of my flights were direct from Seattle or San Francisco)
* premium economy seat (seating arrangement and a slightly-separated cabin area)
* brand (ANA or JAL definitely preferred, but more expensive than others, but using a Japanese airline with Japanese staff, systems, and meals, is a nice way to start the "culture of Japan" experience)
* schedule & cost
