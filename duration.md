# Duration
date: 2023-11-02

I would say 5-7 days in Tokyo would be a minimum. With a shorter trip you could visit many sites in Tokyo, and maybe take a day trip to:
   * Hiking at Takao-san - about 1 hour by train
   * Yokohama - about 1 hour by train
   * Kamakura (sort of like a mini-Kyoto near Tokyo) - about 1-2 hours by train

With only 5 days in Tokyo, you probably don't have enough time to comfortably go further out to places like Kyoto, Osaka, or Kobe.

On the other hand, with a shorter trip in Tokyo, it could be easier to add some other stops like South Korea (Seoul, Busan), Taiwan, China, Thailand, - if that sounds interesting.

With 10-14 days in Japan, you could plan some out of town trips:
   * Kyoto, Osaka, or Kobe (2 nights min I would say) - about 2-3 hours from Tokyo on the Shinkansen (Bullet Train).
   * Hakone - small town feeling hot springs, lake, and old-fashioned/vintage resort area in the countryside (about 2 hours from Tokyo)
   * Sapporo (great in summer travel when Tokyo gets so hot, Sapporo is to the north; like of like going from Austin to Seattle in the summer?)
